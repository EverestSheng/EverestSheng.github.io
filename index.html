I study partial differential equations. In particular, I am interested in equations derived from fluids such as Euler and navier-stokes, and in nonlinear wave and dispersive equations derived from geometry and quantum fields. Currently, I am working on several aspects of PDE:
  • Fluid stability for Euler and Navier-Stokes,
  • Boundary layer and inviscid limits from Navier-Stokes to Euler,
  • Well-posedness and scattering for nonlinear wave equations,
  • Well-posedness and scattering for nonlinear dispersive equations.
