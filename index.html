I study partial differential equations and obtain the Phd degree in Fudan University under the supervision of Prof. Yi Zhou. In addition, I am engaged in postdoctoral research at Peking University under the supervision of Prof. Chao Wang and Zhifei Zhang. In particular, I am interested in the equations describing fluid motion such as Euler and Navier-Stokes, and in nonlinear wave and dispersive equations derived from geometry and quantum fields. Currently, I am working on several aspects of PDE:
  • Fluid stability for Euler and Navier-Stokes,
  • Boundary layer and inviscid limits from Navier-Stokes to Euler,
  • Well-posedness and scattering for nonlinear wave equations,
  • Well-posedness and scattering for nonlinear dispersive equations.
